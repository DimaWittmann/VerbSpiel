# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

## Overview
VerbSpiel stores your learning progress locally on your device. This includes
practice statistics (shown/correct/failed counts), favorites, and learned flags.
The app does not collect, transmit, or share personal data off the device.

## Data We Store
- App activity: practice stats (shown/correct/failed counts, timestamps)
- User selections: favorites and learned words

## Data Sharing
We do not share your data with third parties.

## Data Retention
All data stays on your device. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

## Your Choices
You can reset your progress inside the app to clear local data.
